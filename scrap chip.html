SCRAP CHIP CONVEYOR
The Scrap Conveyors are basically Hinged Steel Belt type and are designed and manufactured as per International Standards.
The Standard Chain pitches are
2.5” (63.5 mm)
4” (101.6 mm)
6” (152.4 mm)
8” (203.2 mm)
9” (229 mm)
Belt widths vary in in progression of 1½’ i.e., from 12” to 54” maximum widths.
Scrap carrying capacity usually is expressed in Tons/ hour.
